# SmartERP PRD (Free Single User Version)

## 1. Product Vision

To build a zero-cost, cloud-based, single-user factory ERP system
similar to Tally but mobile-first and real-time.

---

## 2. Target User

- Factory owner
- Small manufacturer
- Single business owner

Only 1 account system.

---

## 3. Core Requirements

### Product Management
- Add/Edit/Delete product
- Set GST %
- HSN Code
- Stock tracking

---

### Invoice System
- Auto GST
- Auto invoice number
- PDF generation
- Immutable after save
- Real-time price reflection (cached)

---

### Inventory
- Stock auto reduce/increase
- Low stock alert

---

### Expense
- Add expense
- Category
- Date filter
- Monthly summary

---

### Payroll
- Add employee
- Mark salary paid
- Salary history

---

### Dashboard
- Sales
- Purchases
- Expenses
- Salary
- Net profit
- GST summary

---

## 4. Non Functional Requirements

- 100% Free
- Client-heavy logic
- Fast (<2 sec invoice generation)
- Offline support
- Secure authentication
- Data isolation

---

## 5. Success Criteria

- Invoice recalculation instant
- Stock always accurate
- No data corruption
- Free tier limits never exceeded
